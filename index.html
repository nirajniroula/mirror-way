HOW TO PLAY? MIRROR WAY is a simple 'run on track' game, where the player should draw a tracking line remaining with in the track and with mirror effect. Click the PLAY button to start the game.
Click anywhere near the track and drag the red ball.
Remember, the ball should not get out of the track and don't forget the mirror effect too!
